Privacy Policy for IT-Duluk

Last Updated: May 17, 2026

1. Introduction

IT-Duluk is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data. This policy is aligned with the revised Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (nFADP) and, where applicable, the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. Information Collection

We collect the following types of information from our visitors:

  • Personal Information: Information that can identify you, such as your name and email address, collected when you contact us, subscribe to a newsletter, or submit a form.
  • Usage Information: Information about your interactions with our website, such as pages visited, time spent on the site, and links clicked.
  • Technical Information: Device data including your browser type, operating system, and anonymized IP address to ensure website compatibility and security.

3. Information Use and Legal Basis

We process your data based on legitimate interests, contract fulfillment, or explicit consent for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To respond to your inquiries and deliver requested IT tutorials or updates.
  • Website Improvement: To analyze technical performance and optimize user experience.
  • Security: To protect our infrastructure against unauthorized access, malicious activities, and spam.

4. Cookies and Consent Management

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies. We distinguish between strictly necessary cookies (required for the website to function) and non-essential cookies (used for analytics and advertising).

In accordance with Swiss and European privacy standards, non-essential cookies are only deployed after you have provided explicit consent via our cookie consent banner. You can manage your preferences or withdraw your consent at any time by accessing the privacy settings on our site.

5. Third-Party Services

We rely on selected third-party services to operate and improve our website. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data:

  • Google Analytics: Subject to your consent, we use Google Analytics to understand website traffic. Your IP address is anonymized before transmission. Data generated by these cookies may be transmitted to Google servers in the United States. You can opt out directly via our cookie banner or by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • Google AdSense: We may display advertisements using Google AdSense. With your consent, Google uses cookies to serve personalized ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can manage personalized advertising via Google Ads Settings.
  • Hosting Provider: Our website is hosted by GitHub Pages (GitHub, Inc.). GitHub processes technical server log data necessary for the secure delivery of the website. For more details, see the GitHub Privacy Statement.

6. International Data Transfers

Some of our service providers are located outside of Switzerland and the European Economic Area (EEA), including in the United States. We ensure that such transfers are safeguarded by recognized legal mechanisms, such as the Swiss-US Data Privacy Framework or standard contractual clauses.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or to comply with mandatory legal retention periods. Once the data is no longer required, it is securely deleted or fully anonymized.

8. Your Rights

Under the nFADP and GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request the correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request the deletion of your personal data.
  • Withdraw your consent for data processing at any time.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details provided below.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect technical or legal changes. The most current version will always be available on this page, indicated by the "Last Updated" date at the top.
